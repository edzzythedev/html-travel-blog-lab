Trip Highlights

My recent trip to Fire Island National Seashore was an unforgettable escape from the mainland hustle. The park offers a unique blend of wild, protected beaches and charming car-free communities. I spent my days exploring the iconic Fire Island Lighthouse and hiking through the Sunken Forest, a rare maritime forest ecosystem that feels truly magical. The lack of cars creates an incredibly peaceful atmosphere, making the ferry ride over feel like a transition into another world.

Key features included the stunning ocean views, the rustic boardwalks connecting the towns, and the chance to spot local wildlife, particularly the unique deer population. It's the perfect place for a relaxing, nature-focused adventure.
